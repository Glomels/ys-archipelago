## Logic Notes

- **Shrine Key** gates Shrine access
- **Prison Key** gates Mine access (and rescues Feena)
- **Darm Key** gates Tower access
- **Hammer** required to break walls for progression in Tower
- **Rod** required to activate statues in upper Tower
- **Monocle** required for hidden paths (F13+)
- **All 6 Books** required for Blue Amulet from Luta Gemma
- **Blue Amulet** required for Dark Fact fight

Some logic may need refinement based on actual PSP Chronicles version specifics.
